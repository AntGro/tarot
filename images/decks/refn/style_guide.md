# Refn Neon — Style Guide

## Inspiration
Nicolas Winding Refn films: *Drive*, *Only God Forgives*, *The Neon Demon*

## Color Palette
- **Primary**: neon pink/magenta (#FF007F), electric blue (#00BFFF)
- **Background**: deep black, near-black shadows
- **Accents**: chrome silver, blood red splatter
- **Glow**: soft neon haloes around figures and symbols

## Visual Style
- High contrast chiaroscuro lighting
- Hyper-stylized, moody noir atmosphere
- Geometric Art Deco patterns where ornamental detail exists
- Figures are stoic, dangerous, beautiful — cinematic anti-heroes
- Satin/leather textures on clothing
- Blood splatter accents (subtle, not gratuitous)

## Card Layout — STRICT RULES
- **Border**: thin dark blue/navy border around the entire card
- **Top-left corner**: LARGE suit symbol (heart/spade/diamond/club) in neon pink/magenta, with letter (R/D/V) directly BELOW the symbol, also in neon pink/magenta
- **Top-right corner**: smaller letter R only, in dark blue/navy (subtle, secondary)
- **Symmetry**: bottom half is exact 180° rotation of top half (generated programmatically)
- **Background**: deep black/dark blue gradient, NOT white
- **Figure**: centered, occupying most of the card width, lit dramatically

## Symbol Colors (per suit)
- **Hearts ♥**: neon pink/magenta glow
- **Diamonds ♦**: neon pink/magenta glow
- **Spades ♠**: electric blue/dark blue glow
- **Clubs ♣**: electric blue/dark blue glow
- Pink suits (hearts, diamonds) use neon pink for symbols, letters, and accents
- Blue suits (spades, clubs) use electric blue for symbols, letters, and accents

## Character Reinterpretation
- **Kings**: dangerous crime lords / lone wolf drivers, slicked hair, leather or satin jackets, beards, crowns reinterpreted as dark ornate headpieces
- **Queens**: femme fatales, neon-lit, elegant and deadly
- **Jacks**: young enforcers, sharp features, intense gaze
- **Knights**: motorcycle riders, helmeted or masked

## Reference Card
The king_of_hearts establishes the visual standard:
- Bearded king with dark ornate crown
- Ermine/cross-patterned robe with chain details
- Holding scepter in left hand
- Split pink (left) / blue (right) lighting on face
- Large pink heart top-left, pink R below it
- Small dark blue R top-right
